Decision 2 · Why flagged

What actually drives the risk?

Leakage-safe Random-Forest importances. The signal is behavioural, not demographic — no protected attribute reaches the top tier. Switch between SHAP and permutation views.

Read it as

The strongest driver is the proportion of active course weeks — a pure engagement signal — followed by assessment and submission activity. The three Motivation Proxy Index dimensions (purple) enter as incremental, interpretable contributors. Removing leakage barely dents performance (AUC 0.983 → 0.969), confirming the model learns behaviour, not a restated grade.
